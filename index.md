<div class="intro-text-bottom">

Previously, I did my PhD under the supervision of [Laurette Tuckerman](https://blog.espci.fr/laurette/){:target="_blank"} and [Dwight Barkley](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/sci/maths/people/staff/dwight_barkley/home_page/){:target="_blank"}, in Sorbonne Universit√© in Paris ([PMMH lab](https://www.pmmh.espci.fr/-Home-){:target="_blank"}/EPSCI Paris).

</p>

My research interests include:

- Effect of rotation on turbulence
- Perturbative approaches and turbulent closures
- Self-organization of turbulence from waves dynamics
- Shear flows and emergence of turbulence
